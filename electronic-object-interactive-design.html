About the project

Interaction design workshop on "everyday electronic objects" on interactive digital object design.

Problem

Conflicts and tensions between human beings are things that happen depending on the person. Conflict is not something to be avoided because it allows one to move forward and anger, annoyance, insults and so on do not allow one to move forward in this conflict. People who cannot control themselves and communicate will behave in a contradictory way when resolving conflict. We have experimented to accompany conflicts.

Solution

Feel is an application that allows you to control your tensions and anticipate conflict situations in order to improve community life. When the smart bracelet detects that your nerves are on the verge of exploding through your heartbeat, it manifests itself by producing pressure on your wrist and invites you to consult your application. The base with the talking ball activates when it detects that a conflict is breaking out. It invites users to take the talking ball with them in order to continue the confrontation. The ball connected to the bracelet follows the principles of the talking stick to accompany communication.

Ideation

Based on the theme, we started by brainstorming to find and link themes. This is how we came up with the idea of "arguing". It represents everyday moments in contexts, subjects and provokes certain emotions that are likely to happen.

Â 

Scenario

We have scripted contexts of "disputes" and experimented with various mediums. This has allowed us to understand how elements and stimuli outside the person's body that can affect him/her work. We have materialized the results and research to understand why disputes happen, but it also depends on the character of each person.

We sought out many ideas through scriptwriting and brainstorming in order to make them emerge as prototypes.

Â 

Prototyping

The design process is iterative and prototyping allows for the exploration of experiments and objects. Scenarios, experience prototypes and tests have allowed us to orient our path and design choices towards a product that allows mediation and accompaniment in conflictual moments where there may be a lack of communication. In this sense, objects are an intermediary in changing the location of conflict between people.

We made the user journey map of a dispute using the service blueprint tool, imagining the interactions with the ball (light, sound), the wrist (vibration) and the application (notifications). In addition, I designed with Alfiane the information architecture and prototypes of low-fidelity interfaces.

Final products

We chose to experience the dispute in the context of the family home. When a compelling moment occurs, the smart bracelet detects arm movements and pulse, and from a threshold emits a first vibration on the wrist to inform that anger is rising. At the end of the second stressing event, another wrist contraction is sent. Finally, if the tone rises again between the individuals, the base locates the argument and starts flashing to make the individual take the ball and handle it. It becomes the talking stick to accompany the participants.

We diversified our media, from cardboard prototypes, sketches to visual models and I designed 3D prototypes.
